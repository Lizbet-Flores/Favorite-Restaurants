Welcome to my Favorite Restaurants Page!

This site is all about where I love to go out in the valley.

Here are some places I love to travel to:

  • Cocina Madrigal

    • This small restaurant hidden in South Phoenix has the best authentic tacos. I definitely recommend trying their steak or chicken tacos whenever you're feeling something spicy.


  • Phở Thành Restaurant

    • Vietnamese food is my ultimate favorite! This spot is another hidden gem in the valley with a boba & ice cream spot that's open right next door.


  • The Oink Cafe

    • Up next is The Oink Cafe. This spot is a bacon themed breakfast joint. I typically visit the location in North Phoenix, but the Tempe location is not too bad either.


  • Sushiholic

    • The only sushi & Japanese grill I like is located in East Phoenix. This spot has unique rolls & plates.


  • The Kettle Black

    • This is a great Gastropub located in Downtown Phoenix.


  • Red Devil Italian Restaurant

    • Lastly, Red Devil is one of my favorite pizzarias because of their brick oven cooked pies. They also have great cannolis!
